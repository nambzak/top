What is systemd?

systemd claims to be a good modern replacment for SysVinit ‐ a so called daemon. Usually the init daemon is the first process spawned by the kernel and thus has the PID #1 and
is responsible for spawning other daemons which are necessary for the OS to operate, e.g. networking, cron, syslog etc.
